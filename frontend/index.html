Report Your Situation

Use our app or website to describe your emergency, location, and current situation.

  • Easy-to-use form interface
  • Submit photos or voice notes
  • Works offline in emergencies

Authorities Notified

Our system instantly alerts relevant authorities including Nepal Embassy, police, and support organizations.

  • Automatic geo-location based routing
  • SMS, email, and app notifications
  • Confirmation when authorities view your report

Track Response

Monitor which authorities have seen your report and their response actions in real-time.

  • Real-time status updates
  • In-app messaging with responders
  • Timeline of all actions taken

Receive Assistance

Get coordinated help from the appropriate authorities and continue to receive support throughout your case.

  • On-the-ground support coordination
  • Legal aid and documentation assistance
  • Ongoing case management support
